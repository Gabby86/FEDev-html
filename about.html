What is football?

Football is a popular team sport played across the globe, known as soccer in some regions.
It typically involves two teams of 11 players each competing to score goals by getting a
spherical ball into the opposing team's net.

Played on rectangular fields with goals at each end, football combines strategy, skill, and physical prowess.

The primary objective is to control the ball, pass it between teammates, and strategically maneuver it past the
opposing team's defense to score goals while preventing the opposing team from doing the same.

Football fosters teamwork, agility, and endurance, captivating millions of fans and players who
participate at various levels, from casual matches to professional leagues and international tournaments.
